Privacy Policy

Last updated: 08/06/2026

1. Introduction

Medivum is a personal medical notebook for recording vaccinations, doctor visits, reminders, attachments and other health notes. The current version is designed as a local tool that stores data on the user’s device.

2. Data stored by the app

The app may store data entered by the user, including profiles, PIN code, vaccination records, doses and reminders, doctor visits, doctor details, notes, photos, PDF documents, other attachments and app settings.

3. Where data is stored

All data is stored locally on the user’s device. The app does not normally send personal or health data to a remote server. The app operator has no access to data stored on the user’s device.

4. Profiles and PIN

Profiles and PIN are used only locally in the app. The PIN helps restrict access to the app but does not represent full encryption of data stored on the device.

5. App permissions

The app may request access to the camera, photos, files and notifications. These permissions are used for adding attachments and displaying local reminders configured by the user.

6. Data sharing

The app does not share personal or health data with third parties. The app contains no advertising and does not use analytics tools to track users.

7. Security

We recommend using device-level security such as a system PIN, password or biometric authentication. The user is responsible for securing their device and access to the app.

8. Attachments and local files

Attachments added to the app may be copied to the app’s local storage so they remain available even if the original file is removed elsewhere on the device.

9. User rights

Because data is stored locally, users can manage or delete it by deleting individual records, removing attachments or uninstalling the app.

10. GDPR

Because the app does not normally transfer personal data to the operator and data remains on the user’s device, most GDPR rights can be exercised directly by managing or deleting data on the user’s device.

11. Changes

This policy may be updated from time to time. The current version will be available on this page.
